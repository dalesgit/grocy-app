# Watermelon Salad

*vegan*

**4 servings**

---

- *1.5kg* watermelon
- *200g* feta cheese
- *200g* cherry tomatoes
- *30g* pine nuts
- *30g* black olives
- salad of choice
- balsamic vinegar sauce
- ciabatta bread

---

1. Roast pine nuts, wash tomatoes, cut everything into prefered size.
2. Mix and serve with balsamic vinegar sauce and cut ciabatta bread.
